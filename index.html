About Me

I am a fun loving, free spirited and individual self-taught freelance Web Designer.
I like to think that this eclectic mix of personality traits can lead to the creation of unique pieces of work.
I take inspiration from my surroundings, and really try to match my design theme to the personality of my client.
I design with passion for my subject and sensitivity to my clients.

What's my story?

I have a background in travel and customer service. I worked for many years as a flight attendant, and from this, have become adept in building close working relationships with those I meet. I believe that we can view a website as our environment-the way it looks and feels really can influence whether we want to 'be there', or whether we click to a different site.
With this in mind, I like to really get to know my clients and talk about how they want their audience to feel when they land on their homepage, and how the site's environment can be designed to affect this.
